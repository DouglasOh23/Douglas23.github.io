Frequently Asked Questions

What is this service about?

This service is about providing electric cars for rent to customers who want to reduce their carbon footprint and contribute to a cleaner environment.

How do I get started?

To get started, you can visit our website and select the car model you want to rent. Then, choose the pickup location and the dates you want to book it for.

What are the benefits of using this service?

By using our service, you can enjoy a convenient and eco-friendly transportation option. You can rent an electric car for a few hours or a few days, depending on your needs.
